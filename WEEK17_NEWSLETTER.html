Saboner 1988.60 def. Luka my Balls 1909.50

Season series: Nick 5-1 | All-time: Nick leads 45-20

Garrett finally kicked in the door. After fourteen straight losses to Nick, Saboner stole Week 17 with a 1988.60--1909.50 gut punch that somehow felt both shocking and completely earned. The win had three obvious fingerprints. Nick didnt exactly fold. Karl-Anthony Towns led the charge with 207.80 FP in 5 games, Jalen Johnson posted 191.55 FP over 4, and Anthony Edwards chipped in 173.00 FP in 4--but Luka my Balls never fully got into rhythm thanks to 18 games lost to injury. The bigger picture is the part that stings: Garrett has now taken down the leagues top two teams in back-to-back weeks, and hes finally got a season-best 2-game win streak to show for it. Nicks still sitting at 13-4 with the regular-season crown within arms reach, but the automatic aura took a measurable hit--like a five-title dynasty realizing the undercard can, in fact, land haymakers.
